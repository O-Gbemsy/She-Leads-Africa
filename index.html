SLA BoostHer Program

The SLA BoostHer Program, in collaboration with Jobberman is a
transformative initiative designed to empower young Nigerian
women entrepreneurs. This program adresses the unique
challenge faced by women in business and job-seeking through a
blened learning experience that includes advanced self-paced
courses. Through a blended learning approach, participants will gain
access to high quailty training, expert-led masterclasses, and hands-
on guidance tailord on their needs, fostering sustainable business
growth and economic independence
